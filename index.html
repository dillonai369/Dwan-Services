What We Offer

FLOOR SYSTEMS
BUILT TO LAST

System 01
Epoxy Floor Systems

High-build epoxy coatings that bond directly to concrete. Available in solid colors, metallics, and custom blends — garage-ready or showroom-sharp.

  • Seamless, easy-clean surface
  • Chemical & stain resistant
  • High-gloss or satin finish
System 02
Polyurea Coatings

Flexible, ultra-durable polyurea topcoats that outperform standard epoxy. UV-stable and impact-resistant — the professional standard.

  • 4x more impact resistance than epoxy
  • UV-stable — won't yellow
  • Cures fast, back in service sooner
Most Popular
Chip Broadcast

Decorative vinyl flake chips broadcast into the base coat, sealed with a clear polyurea topcoat. The industry standard for garage floors.

  • Dozens of color blends available
  • Hides imperfections beautifully
  • Non-slip texture built in
Most Common
Garage Floors

Transform bare concrete into a showroom-quality surface that's durable, cleanable, and sharp. Single-car to large multi-car garages.

  • Single & multi-car garages
  • Driveways & aprons
  • Full surface prep included
Interior
Basements & Interiors

Sealed, bright, moisture-resistant basement floors ready for living or storage. Patios and sunrooms too — any concrete surface qualifies.

  • Moisture mitigation options
  • Seamless full-room coverage
  • Patios & sunrooms included

Commercial Spaces

High-performance floor systems for warehouses, showrooms, retail, and industrial environments. Heavy-duty formulas built for foot and vehicle traffic.

How It Works

OUR
PROCESS

01
Free Consultation

Brian comes to your space, assesses the floor condition, and gives you a straight quote — no obligation, no pressure, no sales tactics.

02
Surface Prep

We grind and profile the concrete to ensure maximum bond strength. Cracks, spalls, and problem areas are repaired before any coating touches the floor.

03
Coating Application

Base coat goes down, chips are broadcast if applicable, and a protective polyurea topcoat seals everything in. Layered for a flawless, lasting finish.

04
Final Inspection

We walk the completed job with you before we leave. If it's not right, we make it right — every single time, no exceptions.
